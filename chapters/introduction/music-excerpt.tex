\section{Block excerpt (numbered example)}

When a passage deserves the spotlight instead of a parenthesis, you promote it to a numbered example---still plain-text sources, still the same collaboration story, now with a figure number your students can cite.

Printed block examples share one house style: comfortable spacing around the example, each system centered in the text block where that matters, and a running list at the front of the book so readers can find figures by number.

\beginBookExample{C major scale in whole notes}
\beginBookExampleMusic
\lilypondfile{chapters/introduction/excerpt/c-major-scale.ly}
\finishBookExampleMusic
\finishBookExample

Shorter quotations of music slipped into other sections should follow the same pattern: keep the prose in one file and include a dedicated fragment from the inline or excerpt area.
